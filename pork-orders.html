🔍 What is “Hanging Weight”?

That’s the pig’s weight after slaughter, before cutting.
Your final packaged meat will be around 65–75% of the hanging weight, depending on cut and processing preferences.

📦 Cut Options:

  • Pork chops
  • Shoulder & butt roasts
  • Bacon
  • Ham (whole or sliced)
  • Ribs
  • Sausage (breakfast, sweet, or hot)
  • Ground pork
  • Optional: lard, hocks, jowl, soup bones
