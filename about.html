About Roswell Family Farm

Roswell Family Farm is lovingly run by Anthony and Emily Roswell, along with their three kids — Willow (6), Wayon (5), and Wyatt (3). Nestled in Salisbury, NY, our family is passionate about raising happy, healthy animals the natural way.

We specialize in pasture-raised chickens and have added ducks for both meat and eggs. Birds are raised outdoors on fresh pasture, with sunshine, greens, and room to roam — which makes for incredible flavor you can feel good about.

Through our close partnership with Craig and Stephanie Spofford of Spofford Farm, we now offer roaster pigs and pasture-raised pork cuts (plus whole and half hogs). We’ve also expanded to include beef cuts so you can stock your freezer with local, high-quality meats from one trusted place.

Whether you’re enjoying chicken, duck, pork, or beef from Roswell Family Farm, you can trust it’s raised with care, transparency, and a whole lot of heart.

About Roswell Family Farm

Roswell Family Farm is lovingly run by Anthony and Emily Roswell, along with their three kids — Willow (6), Waylon (5), and Wyatt (3). Nestled in Salisbury, NY, our family is passionate about raising healthy animals the right way, and sharing farm-fresh food with our community.

We began with pasture-raised chickens, giving them room to roam, sunshine to soak in, and fresh grass and bugs to enjoy every day. This natural lifestyle makes for happier birds and richer flavor you can taste in every bite. We’ve since added ducks for both meat and eggs, bringing even more variety and farm-fresh goodness straight from our pastures to your plate.

Through our close partnership with Craig and Stephanie Spofford of Spofford Farm, we’re proud to offer roaster pigs, whole and half hogs, and a wide variety of pasture-raised pork cuts. This partnership lets us bring you pork that’s tender, flavorful, and raised with the same values we live by here at Roswell Family Farm.

For our beef, we source directly from trusted nearby family farms. These farms raise cattle on open pastures with the same standards of care, honesty, and respect that we believe in. By sourcing locally, we not only ensure the highest quality cuts but also support other hardworking families in our community. It’s food that strengthens the bonds of local farming while giving you meals that are rich, wholesome, and unforgettable.

Whether it’s chicken, duck, pork, or beef, you can count on Roswell Family Farm to provide food that’s raised with love, rooted in tradition, and bursting with real flavor.
