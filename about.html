About Roswell Family Farm

Roswell Family Farm is lovingly run by Anthony and Emily Roswell, along with their three young kids: Willow (6), Waylon (5), and Wyatt (3). Nestled in Salisbury, NY, their family is passionate about raising happy, healthy animals the natural way.

They specialize in pasture-raised chickens and are excited to expand soon into raising goats as well. Through their close partnership with Craig and Stephanie Spofford of Spofford Farm, the Roswells are also proud to offer delicious, all-natural, pasture-raised pork — available as whole hogs, halves, or (coming soon) individual cuts.

Whether you're enjoying chicken, pork, or goat from Roswell Family Farm, you can trust it's raised with love, care, and a whole lot of heart.
