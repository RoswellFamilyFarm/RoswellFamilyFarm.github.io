Welcome to Roswell Family Farm

Nestled in the heart of Salisbury, NY, Roswell Family Farm is a family-run homestead dedicated to raising animals the right way — on pasture, with care, intention, and plenty of fresh air.

The farm is led by Anthony and Emily Roswell, who are raising their three beautiful children — Willow (6), Wayon (5), and Wyatt (3) — to appreciate the land, animals, and values of real, honest food.

The Roswell family — working together to raise good food with heart.

At the heart of their operation are their happy, healthy pasture-raised chickens — raised with no hormones or antibiotics, just sunshine, bugs, grass, and love. Soon, the Roswells will be introducing goats to their pasture rotation as well!

Beyond their own animals, Anthony and Emily are proud to team up with close friends and fellow farmers Craig and Stephanie Spofford of Spofford Farm. This partnership brings you delicious, all-natural pork — pasture-raised with care, and available as whole hogs, half hogs, or (soon) a variety of convenient cuts.

Willow, Wayon, and Wyatt — the next generation of farmers.

Roswell Family Farm is more than just a place to buy good meat — it’s a place rooted in family, stewardship, and a commitment to doing things the right way. From our family to yours, thank you for supporting local, pasture-raised food.
